Team GECCO

I'm working as part of Team GECCO (Gel Ethanol Cervical Cancer Obliterator), a group of Duke engineering students working towards creating an electromechanical device for the treatment of cervical cancer. The device will inject a gel form of ethanol into the cervix in order to ablate cancerous regions quickly and automatically. The goal of this project is to deliver a clinical-grade product, engineered to rigorous standards.
